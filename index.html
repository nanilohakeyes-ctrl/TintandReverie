My commitment to your hair
"The color is temporary. The integrity of your hair is forever."

Every service includes professional bond repair as a standard — not an upgrade. I use Olaplex and K18 because color without care is just damage dressed up pretty.

1

Pre-color bond prep

Olaplex No.1 or K18 applied before processing to protect bonds while hair is most vulnerable.

2

In-process protection

Bond builder worked directly into the color formula — active the entire processing time.

3

Post-color treatment

Olaplex No.2 or K18 mask applied after rinsing to reconnect broken bonds and seal the cuticle.

4

Protein & moisture finish

Joico-grade protein treatment + leave-in conditioner. Your hair leaves stronger than it arrived.
